# Quantum Models

Quantum many-body and single-particle models with exact or
rigorously verifiable solutions.

## Contents

- [Transverse-Field Ising Model (TFIM)](tfim.md) -- free-fermion
  solution via Jordan--Wigner, BdG spectrum, thermal observables,
  entanglement entropy
- [Heisenberg Chain](heisenberg.md) -- spin-1/2 AFM chain, dimer and
  4-site exact spectra, Bethe ansatz ground-state energy density
- [Tight-Binding Models](tightbinding/index.md) -- nearest-neighbor
  hopping on 8 lattice topologies, with closed-form Bloch spectra and
  a generic builder
